How it works

This voice-assisted chat agent will capture your ideas and automatically add them as posts to the board. You can:

  • Jump to any specific theme
  • Work through each theme in turn
  • Hang up, have a think, then start again anytime

Workshop Themes

Theme 1: Workflow Friction

  1. What's a repetitive task you wish you didn't have to do?
  2. Where do you feel your time is being wasted?
  3. What's one thing you often have to chase people or systems for?

Theme 2: Member Experience

  1. Where do members get stuck, confused, or frustrated?
  2. What could make our service faster or more personalised?

Theme 3: Decision Support / Insight Gaps

  1. What decisions are hard to make due to lack of data or clarity?
  2. If you could get one new insight instantly, what would it be?

Theme 4: Wishlist / Future Vision

  1. If you had a smart assistant that understood your role, what would you want it to do for you?
  2. If you could change one system or process overnight, what would it be?
